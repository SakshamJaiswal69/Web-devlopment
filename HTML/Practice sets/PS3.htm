Apple’s innovative trajectory is something that every tech enthusiast wants to learn about and I was all in for it. Initially I couldn’t get through in 2019 for the student scholarship at WWDC, but I kept at it, working and applying consistently when March 28th’s email notification for the invite to WWDC 24 threw me off my feet. Representing Blinkit at my dream conference along with my project, this couldn’t get any better.
